Privacy Policy / Politique de Confidentialité

Last updated: January 2026

WaterMind is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to be fully offline and secure.

1. Data Collection

WaterMind does not collect, store on external servers, or share any personal information. We have no access to your data.

2. Local Storage

All your hydration data (daily intake, goals, and history) is stored locally on your device using secure internal storage. This data never leaves your phone.

3. Notifications

The app uses local reminders to help you stay hydrated. These notifications are triggered by your phone's system and do not involve any data tracking or external processing.

4. Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can reset your history within the app settings. Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data from your device.
